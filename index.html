Welcome

Hello, world! My name is Christopher Bueker. This is my developer hub: a place to document tools, projects, and my open-source computer science and information technologies education, and to connect my technical work across SEO, web infrastructure, and cybersecurity.

With nearly a decade of experience in counseling, I originally built a life coaching website; and through that process, I was led into technical SEO, web infrastructure, and cybersecurity.

Today, I consult businesses and ministries through cybersecurity, web infrastructure, and technical SEO. My business values include integrity, excellence, customer service, innovation, and providing excellent value. I’m here to help your business succeed online with clarity, precision, and strategy.
