Curriculum for My Degree

This curriculum is the result of years of dedication, reflection, and practice. As I pursued mastery in software engineering, web development, and SEO, I discovered that my body of knowledge had grown to resemble a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Web Technologies.

Inspired by the OSSU (Open Source Society University) curriculum and the CS2013 Guidelines, I’ve structured my self-taught degree to meet and exceed the standards of an accredited undergraduate program — with a strong emphasis on real-world skills, applied projects, and ethical development.

This page will outline the areas of study, the core and advanced topics, and the unique competencies I’ve developed along this journey.
